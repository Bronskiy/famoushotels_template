Searched & Found

Established to create an exclusive group of individual travellers, THE FRIENDS of THE MOST FAMOUS HOTELS IN THE WORLD is a circle of well travelled people who have signed up for our free newsletter and have become 'Corresponding Members'.

We have a passion for travel, a love for historic hotels and we simply want to share with you our sense of global thinking. Our team is constantly on the outlook of interesting news to make your journey a bit more interesting.

You too are invited to become a Corresponding Member.

Benefits: As a Friend you will receive : personal inviations to our book launching parties all over the world : our regular newsletter : updates on travel related medical news

Be the first to know - join the club!

Become a Member

The Friends of The Most Famous Hotels is a global non-profit organization. Although we do have full membership schemes available, at first, you might like to become a corresponding member so as to receive regular updates on our activities.

Please introduce yourself to our club committee and join us as a corresponding member. Corresponding membership is free of charge and doesn't commit you to anything. After filling in your name and e-mail address you will receive automatically our regular newsletter.
